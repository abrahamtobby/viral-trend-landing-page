How do I register either as an advertiser or a promoter?

Hey! So you wish to go viral or earn some cool cash performing some social task with viral trend, but don't know how to go about registration?

Don't beat yourself too much, registration is quite simple and website is user friendly.

To register all you need to do is visit our website https://www.viraltrnd.org Please ensure that you choose 'promote and earn' while registering to start performing tasks or you can choose 'Advertiser' to publish adverts.

Once you have registered, the website will auto atically send you a verification link to the email address that you registered with.

Once you recieve the verification link, login into your email and click on 'verify' email. Choose a package, basic or premium account type, pay and you are now good to go!

Simple right?

How do I link my social media account to my viral trend account?

So you have sucessfully registered with the Viral trend? Congratulations!

But you are now confused on how to link your social media accounts to your viral trend account?

Well, it is not hard as you may think!

To link your social media accounts you would need to first log in to your social media accounts to the site which includes Facebook, Twitter, Youtube, and Instagram accounts into different tabs in your browser (Firefox/chrome).

Then go to your viral trend dashboard, click on profile and connect your social media usernames correctly to the site.

However, you should note that you can always leave any social media network which you don't want to link.

NOTE:

Be informed that such action would make you not to be seeing tasks from that particular social media from Viraltrend dashboard.

You can also skip thte linking and continue from your profile when you are done with your registration.

Please note that you must link only your active social media accounts with our platform as nonactive or dormant accounts are not accepted on our platform and when used on our platfrom that can lead to our team banning your account.

How to perform social media tasks such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube.

Performing social medai tasks is quite simple on viral trend

To effectively do that, you need to ensure that your instagram account is active in your browser/ app.

After that go to your dashboard, and click 'perform' once you see instagram tasks, then input your email and username to perform the task.

Simple right?

Firstly try logging out and logging in from your instagram account or you can try changing your instagram password or just switch to another instagram account.

if the problem persists, you can contact an Admin to change your instagram account. Usually, the action block lasts for about 3-7 days.
